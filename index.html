Mansaf

"Mansaf, the crown jewel of Al-ع cuisine, epitomizes the regal essence of hospitality and culinary mastery. Picture a sumptuous feast fit for royalty, where tender lamb, marinated in a symphony of aromatic spices, is slow-cooked to perfection in a velvety bath of tangy yogurt infused with saffron and hints of cardamom.

This majestic creation is then lavishly served atop a bed of fragrant, long-grain rice, each grain shimmering with the essence of tradition and opulence.

Murgh Musallam

Murgh Musallam, a jewel in the crown of royal Al-ع cuisine, epitomizes culinary opulence and sophistication. This majestic dish, steeped in centuries of tradition and refinement, features a whole succulent chicken, marinated in a regal blend of aromatic spices and tenderizing agents fit for a king's palate.

Adorned with a cloak of rich, creamy almond gravy and garnished with strands of golden saffron, slivers of silvered almonds, and fragrant rose petals of royal dining.
